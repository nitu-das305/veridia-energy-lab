Our Story

We Started With a Simple Question:
Why Are We Still Burning Carbon?

The world has two carbon problems that actually cancel each other out — too much CO₂ in the atmosphere, and too much waste biomass going to landfills and incinerators. Veridia Energy was founded to solve both, simultaneously.

Why Veridia Exists

Aviation cannot be electrified. The chemicals industry cannot run on sunlight. Steel, cement, and plastics require carbon feedstocks. These are the hardest corners of the decarbonisation challenge — and the ones most investors and startups avoid.

We don't avoid them. Our founders built their careers in precisely these areas: carbon capture systems, biomass conversion chemistry, catalytic process design, and AI-accelerated molecular discovery. Veridia is the company we built to put that expertise to work at scale.

We are headquartered in Navi Mumbai, operate at the intersection of world-class science and industrial pragmatism, and we measure success not in papers published but in tonnes of fossil carbon displaced.

Our Vision

Our Solutions

Six Ways We're Replacing
Fossil Carbon

Each solution is a distinct value stream. Together, they form a closed-loop system that converts waste into value across the entire carbon cycle.

01
Aviation · Fuel

CO₂ to Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

Aviation accounts for a significant share of hard-to-abate emissions — and it cannot go electric. We convert captured CO₂ directly into SAF: a drop-in jet fuel that works in every aircraft flying today, requires no new airport infrastructure, and cuts lifecycle emissions dramatically versus conventional kerosene. Our process is designed around existing carbon capture facilities at industrial sites, turning CO₂ from a compliance cost into a fuel revenue stream.

Relevant for: Airlines · Fuel distributors · Refineries · SAF mandate compliance
Flagship
02
Biorefinery · Chemicals

Biomass to Bio-based Chemicals

Agricultural residues, forestry waste, and energy crops are composed of cellulose, hemicellulose, and lignin — nature's own carbon-rich feedstocks. We fractionate these materials into fermentable sugars and aromatic building blocks, then convert them into platform chemicals that replace petroleum-derived equivalents across paints, adhesives, pharmaceuticals, and polymers. The same tonne of biomass that was previously burned for heat becomes a source of speciality chemical revenue.

Relevant for: Specialty chemical companies · Agricultural sector · Circular economy programs
Core
03
Materials · Bio-composites

Biomass to Advanced Materials

Beyond chemicals, biomass streams — particularly lignin — are precursors to high-performance materials including carbon fibres, bio-based polyols for foams and coatings, and functionalised carbon additives for composites. We develop these materials with performance specifications matched to existing industrial grades, enabling substitution without re-engineering downstream products.

Relevant for: Packaging · Construction · Automotive composites · Electronics
Core
04
CCUS · Decarbonisation

Carbon Capture & Closed-Loop Utilisation

Carbon capture is only half the answer — the other half is what you do with the CO₂. Our CCUS solutions are designed for industrial integration: capturing emissions from power plants, cement, steel, and chemical facilities, and feeding that CO₂ directly into our SAF and chemicals pathways. The result is a closed-loop system where industrial CO₂ never reaches the atmosphere, and instead becomes product. We also optimise capture systems themselves — minimising energy penalties and solvent degradation to make CCUS economically viable without subsidies.

Relevant for: Power utilities · Cement & steel · Chemical complexes · Carbon credit markets
Enabler
05
AI · Process Design

AI-Accelerated Process Development

The traditional approach to process development — trial, error, and iteration in the lab — is slow and expensive. We have built AI and machine learning models that predict how reactions will behave, which catalysts will perform best, and what process conditions will deliver the highest yield and purity, before a single experiment begins. This isn't a research tool. It's an industrial R&D platform that compresses development timelines and dramatically reduces the cost of finding the right answer.

Relevant for: Corporate R&D labs · CROs · Partners seeking faster technology development
Platform
06
Simulation · Validation

Molecular-Level Process Validation

Before any process is built at scale, we validate it at the atomic level — using advanced simulation to understand exactly why it works, where the failure modes are, and how it will perform under industrial conditions. This de-risks scale-up, reduces pilot plant costs, and gives our partners confidence that what works in the lab will work in the plant. It is the scientific foundation that underpins every solution Veridia delivers.

Relevant for: All industry partners · Licensing due diligence · Regulatory submissions
